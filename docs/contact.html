Frequently Asked Questions

What is MediSlim? ▼
MediSlim is a clinical weight management platform that combines evidence-based medicine with AI-powered personalization. It helps healthcare professionals deliver better outcomes for patients managing weight-related conditions.
What clinical guidelines does MediSlim follow? ▼
MediSlim integrates guidelines from the ADA (American Diabetes Association), AACE (American Association of Clinical Endocrinology), and WHO obesity management protocols to ensure evidence-based care.
Does MediSlim support GLP-1 medications? ▼
Yes, MediSlim includes comprehensive GLP-1 agonist tracking and provides medication-adjusted recommendations for nutrition, exercise, and overall treatment plans.
Is MediSlim open source? ▼
Yes, MediSlim is fully open source under the MIT license. You can find the source code on our GitHub repository and contribute to the project.
